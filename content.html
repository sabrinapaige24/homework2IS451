Fun Facts about Bunnies

Bunnies are amazing creatures with lots of interesting facts. Here are a few fun facts about bunnies:

  • Bunnies have 28 teeth and they never stop growing!
  • A group of bunnies is called a "fluffle".
  • Bunnies can jump as high as 36 inches or more.
  • They have a nearly 360-degree field of vision but are born blind.
  • Bunnies purr when they are contented, just like cats!
